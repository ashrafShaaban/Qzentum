OUR WORKSPACE

QZentrum cares about its employees as they make the company what it is – a world leader in software testing and quality assurance services. That is why providing healthy, pleasant and convenient working environment is among QZentrum first priorities.

Working environment in QZentrum satisfies local and international requirements.
