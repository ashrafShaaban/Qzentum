The DevOps philosophy has revolutionized software development by emphasizing collaboration, automation, and continuous integration. DevOps practices optimize the development lifecycle by encouraging developers and operations engineers to work together to help build high-quality applications quickly. With DevOps, organizations achieve greater agility, efficiency, and innovation, which helps businesses thrive.

How can you understand that you need DevOps services and solutions?

To reveal the need for additional DevOps efforts, assess your company's current versus desired maturity level across software development stages. As a rule, five levels of DevOps maturity are differentiated:

01 initial phase

You might lack awareness of DevOps and its potential advantages. Your development and operations teams work independently.

02 Repeatable level

Your company has grasped the fundamental principles of DevOps and is transitioning its focus toward development agility and initial automation within operations.

03 Defined level

Your company initiates transformation by structuring processes around projects/products and implementing automation.

04 Managed level

All environments are efficiently managed, with application metrics consistently collected. The delivery cycles are predictable and frequent.

05 Optimized level

All procedures are entirely automated, with accomplishments easily visible. A system is inherently scalable and adaptable to evolving requirements.
